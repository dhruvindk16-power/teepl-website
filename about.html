About Us

Company Profile

Technicad Edutech & Engineers Pvt. Ltd. (TEEPL) is an engineering consultancy firm based in Vadodara, Gujarat. Established with a high vision to serve the engineering field, TEEPL delivers multi-disciplinary services with a focus on quality, timeliness, and cost-effectiveness.

Located just 5 km from Vadodara Airport, our organization is managed by a team of experienced engineers and technicians who are committed to exceeding client expectations. We specialize in steel structures, piping, civil infrastructure, and offer services across industries like power, process, and chemical.

Our Mission

To provide quality, timely, and economical engineering solutions using modern software tools and an expert team of professionals.

Our Values

  • Excellence in engineering services
  • Customer-centric approach
  • Commitment to deadlines
  • Ethical business practices
